About me

I'm a mechanical engineer with over 15 years of experience designing and delivering complex hardware systems. My background spans aerospace, robotics, electrochemistry, and medical devices. I have led multidisciplinary engineering teams, driven projects from concept to deployment, and built a track record of solving hard problems through first-principles thinking and hands-on execution.

My experience includes: - Technical Lead for the upper stage systems of a low earth orbit launch vehicle - Design, non-linear FEA, and production delivery of a cryogenic pressurized structure - Design, analyses, and delivery of a stage separation mechanism - Design, analyses, and prototyping of a two speed gearbox with only one actuator - Engineering manager for cross-functional teams (up to 12 total reports)

Services offered

- Mechanical Design (CAD, drawing packages, design for manufacturing, design for assembly) - Analysis (hand calculations, structural and modal FEA, advanced modeling) - Prototyping & Test (rapid prototyping, test setup design, test execution) - System engineering (Documentation, requirements definition, risks management, validation) - Project Support (project planning, technical leadership, project management)
